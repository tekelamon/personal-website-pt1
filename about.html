Star Wars

I've always been a major Star Wars nerd. My first experience was wathcing Revenge of the Sith with my mother when I was 6. Since then it's been a love bordering on obsession; from playing the games, watching the shows and movies, to reading comics. My favorite game is Knights of the Old Republic and my favorite movie is Revenge of the Sith. It started the Star Wars love for me. And who doesn't love the Obi-Ani duel?

Video Games

If the Star Wars paragraph didn't warn you, I'm a huge nerd. My first experience with a computer was my Dad's old dinosaur of a computer collecting dust in his office. It never quite interested me until one day he brought home a copy of DOOM. We stayed up all night slaying extremely pixelated demons and definitely didn't get in any trouble with my Mom.

From there I had to know what magic turned that dusty old box into the coolest thing man ever made. I started to take the computer apart looking for it's secrets - I had to know how it worked. Before learning about the world of software development, I would explore the limitations of games. I would test them in different ways to see what worked and what would make them break. I had no idea that it would only be the beginning for me.

Ever since, I kept learning more about each game, each gaming console, each computer. I took each one apart and eventually got to testing mods and trying to change the games. I learned about programming from friends and fell in love. It's all my Dad's fault, I could have been cool...
